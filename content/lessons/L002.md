# Lesson 002 — Applications of Chips

**Level:** Beginner  
**Duration:** 10–15 minutes  

---

## Concept Overview
Chips perform computation, control, and communication inside electronic systems.  
Without chips, modern electronics would not exist.

---

## Industry Applications
- Consumer electronics
- Automotive systems
- Data centers and AI
- Medical devices
- Industrial automation

---

## Key Takeaways
- Chips power all modern devices
- Different applications need different chip designs
- VLSI engineers design application-specific silicon

---

## Self-Test
1. Name two applications of chips.  
2. Why are AI chips different from mobile chips?

---

## Solutions
1. Smartphones, cars, data centers  
2. AI chips focus on parallel processing
