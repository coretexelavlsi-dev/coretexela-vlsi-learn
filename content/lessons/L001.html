Lesson 001 – Why VLSI?

VLSI (Very Large Scale Integration) is the technology that allows millions to billions of transistors to be fabricated on a single silicon chip.

Modern electronics such as smartphones, computers, and AI systems exist because of VLSI technology.

Why VLSI is Important

  • Reduces size and power consumption
  • Improves performance
  • Drives semiconductor industry growth
