L001: Introduction to Digital Electronics

Digital electronics is the foundation of all modern computing systems. It operates using binary signals rather than continuous values.

Concept Intuition

Digital systems work with only two states: 0 and 1.

Formal Definition

Digital Electronics is the study of electronic systems that use discrete signal levels to represent information.

Worked Example

  • OFF → 0
  • ON → 1
