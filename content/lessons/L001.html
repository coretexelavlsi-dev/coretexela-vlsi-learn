Lesson 001 – Why VLSI?

VLSI (Very Large Scale Integration) allows millions of transistors to be placed on a single chip.

This technology is the backbone of modern electronics like smartphones, computers, and AI systems.

Key Points

  • Foundation of semiconductor industry
  • Enables compact and powerful systems
  • High demand career skill
