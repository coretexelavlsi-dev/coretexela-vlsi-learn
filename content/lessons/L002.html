L002: Logic Gates

Logic gates are the basic building blocks of all digital circuits. They operate on binary inputs to produce a binary output.

Concept Intuition

Logic gates perform simple logical decisions such as AND, OR, and NOT. Complex digital systems are built by combining these simple gates.

Formal Definition

A logic gate is an electronic circuit that implements a Boolean function.

Basic Gates

  • AND Gate
  • OR Gate
  • NOT Gate

Worked Example

AND Gate: Output is 1 only when both inputs are 1.

Checkpoint

Q: Which gate inverts its input?

A: NOT gate.
